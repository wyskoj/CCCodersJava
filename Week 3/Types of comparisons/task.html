Making comparisons

In Java, we can compare numbers using these operators:

  • == — Equal to
  • < — Less than
  • > — Greater than
  • <= — Less than or equal to
  • >= — Greater than or equal to
  • != — Not equal to
Note: to compare if two numbers are equal, we have to use TWO equals signs (==). In Java, one equals sign and two equals signs mean two different things.
